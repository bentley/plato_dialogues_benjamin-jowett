Colophon

Dialogues
was compiled from works dating to the 4th century BC attributed to
Plato.
It was translated from Greek in 1892 by
Benjamin Jowett.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks project
by
Jacob Heybey,

Sue Asscher, David Widger, and The Online Distributed Proofreading Team
for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans from the
Internet Archive (Volume 1, Volume 2, Volume 3, Volume 4, and Volume 5).

L’Ecole de Platon,
a painting completed in 1898 by
Jean Delville.

League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
